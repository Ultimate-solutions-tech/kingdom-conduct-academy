Kingdom Conduct Academy

Identity to Impact — A Kingdom Influence Coaching Program

Vision Statement

To raise a generation of Christ-centered individuals who reflect godly character and influence the world for the Kingdom.

Mission Statement

To equip and mentor individuals through biblical teaching and practical tools that build identity, character, and Kingdom influence across the globe.

Course Overview

This 4-week coaching course is designed to equip participants (ages 13–40) with biblical truths, self-awareness, emotional maturity, and practical tools to help them discover their identity in Christ, develop Christlike character, and step into their unique Kingdom purpose and impact.

Program Summary

  • Target Age: 13–40 (teens, young adults, emerging leaders)
  • Duration: 4 Weeks
  • Delivery Format: Online (weekly sessions, assignments, weekend reflections)
  • Facilitator: Pastor B

Additional Training Details

  • Two Core Sessions Weekly (60–90 minutes each)
  • Reflection Journal Prompts
  • One-on-One Coaching Slots
  • Weekend Assignment
  • Devotional Readings
  • Testimonies & Live Reviews (end of week)

The Identity to Impact Journey

  1. WEEK 1 — IDENTITY

    Theme: Who Am I? Discovering Your Identity in Christ

    Objective: To help participants gain a clear, biblical understanding of who they are in Christ, and the foundation of their worth, identity, and uniqueness.

    Topics Covered

    • Created in His Image: Understanding your God-given identity
    • Beyond Labels: Breaking off lies and false identities
    • Accepted and Adopted: What it means to be a child of God
    • The Power of Identity: How it influences your choices, confidence, and direction

    Practical Tools & Activities

    • Self-awareness exercises
    • Personal reflection worksheet: “Who does God say I am?”
    • Scripture meditation guide
    • Identity declaration/confession templates

  2. WEEK 2 — CHARACTER

    Theme: Inside Out — Building Godly Habits and Emotional Maturity

    Objective: Teach the importance of inner transformation, emotional control, and spiritual disciplines as marks of Christlike character.

    Topics Covered

    • The Fruit of the Spirit: Evidence of Christ in you
    • Emotions and Spirituality: Managing feelings without being ruled by them
    • Building godly habits: Prayer, Bible study, service, and discipline
    • Handling temptation and growth through trials

    Practical Tools & Activities

    • Habit tracker template
    • Journaling guide: Character checks
    • Scenario-based reflection: “What would Jesus do?”
    • Accountability partnership setup

  3. WEEK 3 — CHARACTER

    Theme: Walking the Talk — Living a Life that Matches Your Faith

    Objective: Move from internal transformation to external representation. To help participants align their lifestyle with their identity in Christ.

    Topics Covered

    • Integrity and consistency: When no one is watching
    • Relationships that reflect Christ (family, friends, dating)
    • Digital identity and lifestyle: Social media as a testimony
    • Dealing with peer pressure and worldly culture

    Practical Tools & Activities

    • Lifestyle audit
    • Group discussion: “What does Kingdom culture look like?”
    • Social media evaluation tool
    • Role-play exercises: Navigating real-life challenges

  4. WEEK 4 — IMPACT

    Theme: You Were Born for More — Finding and Fulfilling Your Purpose

    Objective: Empower participants to recognise their unique calling and learn how to influence others for Christ in everyday life.

    Topics Covered

    • Understanding purpose from a Kingdom perspective
    • Gifts, talents, and calling: What is in your hand?
    • Making impact in school, work, business, and community
    • Being salt and light: Evangelism and leadership

    Practical Tools & Activities

    • Purpose discovery worksheet
    • Vision board exercise
    • Personal mission statement draft
    • Impact plan (next steps) worksheet

Meet the Facilitators

Pastor Blessing George-Igbinidu (Pastor B)

Pastor Blessing George-Igbinidu is a visionary senior pastor at God’s Word Foundation and Prayer Ministry, which she co-founded with her husband, Rev. George Imafidon Igbinidu, in Benin City, Nigeria.

She holds degrees in Public Administration, Biblical Theology, and Christian Counseling from institutions in Nigeria and Canada.

Driven by a fierce passion for empowering individuals to succeed and live their best lives, Pastor Blessing has authored inspiring books and organised transformative seminars.

She is a passionate advocate for the vital role women play as helpers and leaders in their homes, communities, and society at large. As such, she is the founder and president of the Dorcas Fellowship Foundation.

Additionally, through the Esochris Initiative, she provides vital support to the less privileged, fostering positive change in their lives.

Pastor Blessing hosts the annual “A Date with Pastor B” conference on Valentine’s Day, where she shares insightful teachings on relationships, career building, and spirituality for teenagers and young adults.

She is a devoted partner to her husband and supports him in their joint ministry. They are blessed with two wonderful children, Gift and Christian.

Reverend George I. Igbinidu

Reverened George I. Igbinidu is the Senior Pastor of God’s Word Foundation and Prayer Ministry—popularly known as God’s Word Church. Which he co-founded alongside his beloved wife in the historic city of Benin, Edo State, Nigeria.

A dynamic teacher, visionary leader, and passionate minister of the Gospel. Rev. George holds a Diploma and Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting from the University of Benin. He further honed his leadership capacity through both Basic and Advanced training in Strategic Leadership from the Miracle Invasion Leadership Institute (MILI).

Driven by a deep hunger for God’s Word, he earned a Diploma, Postgraduate Diploma, and a Master’s Degree in Biblical Theology. He is also a business coach and seasoned motivational speaker, drawing from years of corporate experience in a leading multinational organization.

Anointed and divinely called, Rev. George is commissioned with a life-transforming mandate of “Raising Life Champions in the Kingdom of God.” His teachings are known for their depth, clarity, and relevance to daily living.

He is happily married to Rev. Blessing George-Igbinidu, and their union is blessed with two wonderful children, Gift and Christian George-Igbinidu.
