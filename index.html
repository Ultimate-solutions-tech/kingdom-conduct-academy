About Kingdom Conduct Academy

“To equip and mentor individuals through biblical teaching and practical tools that build identity, character, and Kingdom influence across the globe.”

At Kingdom Conduct Academy, we believe transformation begins with identity. When you know who you are in Christ, you carry a confidence that shapes your choices, character, and influence.

Whether you’re a teenager navigating self-discovery or an adult seeking renewed clarity and purpose, this program helps you grow from the inside out—grounded in truth, rooted in character, and ready to impact the world for Christ.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of Christ-centered individuals who reflect godly character and exercise Kingdom influence in every sphere of society.

The Identity to Impact Journey

This is not just another Bible study.
This is a 4-week, intensive coaching program for believers aged 13 – 40, designed to take you from knowing about God to living out His Kingdom culture in every area of life.

  1. WEEK 1 — IDENTITY

    Theme: Who am I? Discovering your Identity in Christ

    Find your worth, security, and direction in the truth of God’s Word. .

    • Created in His Image
    • Beyond Labels & Lies
    • Accepted & Adopted by God
    • The Power of Identity

    Tools: Reflection worksheets, identity declarations, self-awareness exercises.

  2. WEEK 2 — CHARACTER

    Theme: Walking the Talk – Living a Life that Matches Your Faith

    From private conviction to public witness.

    • Fruit of the Spirit
    • Managing Emotions Biblically
    • Building Godly Habits
    • Growing Through Trials

    Tools: Habit trackers, journaling guides, accountability systems.

  3. WEEK 3 — CHARACTER (Part 2)

    Theme: Walking the Talk – Living a Life that Matches Your Faith

    From private conviction to public witness.

    • Integrity & Consistency
    • Relationships that Reflect Christ
    • Social Media & Digital Testimony
    • Resisting Peer Pressure

    Tools: Lifestyle audits, role-play exercises, social media evaluations.

  4. WEEK 4 — IMPACT

    Theme: You Were Born for More – Finding & Fulfilling Your Purpose

    Discover your calling and learn to influence your sphere for Christ.

    • Purpose from a Kingdom Perspective
    • Using Gifts & Talents for God’s Glory
    • Impact in School, Work, Business, and Community
    • Evangelism & Servant Leadership

    Tools: Purpose discovery worksheets, vision boards, personal mission statement drafts, impact plans.

Meet the Speaker

Rev. Mrs. Blessing George-Igbinidu

Lead Coach, Kingdom Conduct Academy

Rev. Mrs. Blessing George-Igbinidu, fondly called Pastor B, is a happily married wife and mother of two. She is the founder of the Dorcas Fellowship Foundation, dedicated to raising women who help others, and the Life Builder Empowerment Organization, which equips people to live purposefully and make a godly impact.

Pastor B is also the visionary behind Kingdom Conduct Academy, a certified counselor, and a transformational coach with a passion for helping Christians discover their identity in Christ, develop godly character, and align their behavior with their beliefs. She holds a Master’s degree in Christian Leadership and Management obtained in Nigeria.
